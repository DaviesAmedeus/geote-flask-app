Mapping Plastics Waste to enhance municipal waste management and disaster management in Morogoro Municipal.

GeoTE In collaboration with the SMCoSE YouthMappers from Sokoine University of Agriculture, we conducted a plastic waste mapping project in Morogoro Municipality to contribute efforts towards improving waste management and reducing flooding effects caused by the piling of plastic waste in waterways. Over 10,000 datasets were collected, analyzed, and the results disseminated in collaboration with the local government.

COMPLETED

Mapping flood protection zones and evacuation routes to improve response capabilities in Morogoro Municipal and Ifakara Town Council

Enthused with improving disaster management and responses, GeoTE conducted a participatory project between local government, community, and affected and interested stakeholders in Morogoro Municipal and Ifakara Town Council, addressing and developing cost-effective ways to improve responses to flooding effects. Data was collected, guideline maps were developed, and new knowledge was developed, contributing to efforts of mitigating flooding effects in project areas.

Open Data Day

Through campaigning and contributing to open-source resources and open data uses, GeoTE participates and engages the community in understanding the power of open data and open-source tools in supporting efforts towards the attainment of the 2030 SDGs. GeoTE has worked with donors and stakeholders to make sure the Tanzanian community can make use of open data through training, workshops, and participatory activities.
